# Code of Conduct

Adopting a concise form of the Contributor Covenant:

- Respect & Inclusion: Harassment, discrimination, hate speech are prohibited.
- Constructive Collaboration: Welcome differing opinions; discuss with civility.
- Reporting: Use GitHub Issues or the email listed in SECURITY.md.

Maintainers may edit/remove inappropriate content or ban accounts when necessary for community safety.

For full guidelines refer to the official Contributor Covenant: https://www.contributor-covenant.org
